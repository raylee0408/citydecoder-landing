Questions?

Do I need to download an app?

No app needed! The game runs entirely in your phone's browser. Just open the link, log in, and play.

Can 1 person play alone?

Absolutely! You can play solo or with up to 6 people. Each player follows on their own phone and the game syncs across all devices in real time.

What if we get stuck on a puzzle?

Each puzzle has a hint button (+1 min penalty) and a background story button. There's also a GPS skip button if you have location issues.

When does my code expire?

Your team code never expires — use it whenever you're ready, rain or shine, any day of the year.

Can I get a refund?

We offer refunds if requested before the game has been started. Contact us at hello@playcitydecoder.com.
